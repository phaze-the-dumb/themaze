Everyone is trying to escape the maze on their own, but you can trade places with a random person every 5 seconds.

Get you and your partner out of the maze as quickly as possible before anyone else, you can trade places with them every 5 seconds. REQUIRES AN EVEN NUMBER OF PLAYERS TO PLAY!

Currently Selected: Solo


Information

How To Play.
Use WASD or click and drag to move around. There are two game mode which are explained in the server lobby. The goal is to get the the finish faster than everyone else

Known Bugs.
- Sometimes the exit will spawn in places it is not supposed to be making the maze impossible
- Cloudflare sometimes times out the websocket connection if no data is sent across it
- Sometimes players don't leave servers properly, resulting in "ghost players"
- The collision system needs work

If you encounter any of these reload and try joining or creating a new server
